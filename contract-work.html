Services and Pricing

Design of a Website

Web design is the first stage of turning the idea you have for a website into a reality. During this stage, designers use a method referred to as wireframing. Wireframing, in its simplest terms, is a rough draft where you lay out all the ideas you have on paper as well as the message you are trying to communicate to your intended audience. Once that is complete, the Designer uses all this information to create a visually pleasing layout for your website and ensure the content is appropriately placed to tell your story.

With the Web Design package, you will receive JPEG images for each page of your site. From there, you can work with a programmer such as myself to have the code created. Another option would be to use the wireframing as a guide to assist you in building your website on a platform such as WordPress or Wix.

The cost for the Web Design service ranges depending on the number of pages and complexity of the site but starts at $4,000.00 for up to a 7-page website.

Code for a Website

The second phase of the website development process is coding. Coding is the process of turning wireframing into reality. There are two sides to the coding process, Front-End, and Back-End. Front-End Developers use HTML, CSS, and Javascript programming languages to code everything a user will see when they visit your site. Back-End Developers use PHP, Ruby, Python, and other programming languages to create the server-side of your website. The server-side covers everything not seen by users who visit your site, but that makes your website function.

As a professional Front-End Developer, I can create all the code necessary for the Front-End of your website. With this package, you will receive all the required Front-End code (HTML, CSS, and Javascript) for your site. This option is excellent for people who want a professional website build, and want to work with a Back-End Developer or another company for the server-side of your site.

The cost for the service ranges depending on the number of pages and complexity of the site but starts at $4,400.00 for up to a 7-page website.

It seems like what was cutting edge just last year is no longer in style as we continuously strive for newer, bigger, and faster tech. Keeping your website up to date is critical. Not only do you need the most updated information on your website as far as products and services you offer are concerned, but you also need an updated design, look, and feel to your site.
